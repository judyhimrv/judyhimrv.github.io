![No Kings 3.jpeg](/uploads/No%20Kings%203.jpeg)On March 28, residents of the Mad River Valley will join millions across the country to tell the current federal administration loud and clear: 

### No Thrones. No Crowns.#NoKings in America! 

Indivisible Mad River Valley will host a  rally in Waitsfield on Main Street, centered by the gazebo on the Mad River Green, from 9:30am-10:30am.  Following our local rally, you can  join with surrounding Indivisible chapters and VPIRG for  a rally on the statehouse lawn in Montpelier, 12noon - 2pm, or see all events nationwide @ [https://www.nokings.org/](https://www.nokings.org/).

In a Democracy, the power resides with the people, not with an authoritarian ruler or regime. Ensuring our safety and freedoms will take ALL of us working together to protect one another. So bring your friends and family, your signs & American flags,  to empower each other and stand for justice.
