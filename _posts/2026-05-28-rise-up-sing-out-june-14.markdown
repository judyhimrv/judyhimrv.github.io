### On June 14, the Committee for the First Amendment (CFA) is hosting Rise Up, Sing Out: 

A Concert for the First Amendment, an uplifting evening of song, solidarity, and action, with support from the No Kings Coalition. Featuring an all-star line-up of performers (Jane Fonda, Patti Smith, Bette Midler, Rufus Wainwright, Sasha Allen, Joy Reid, Wilson Cruz, Broadway Inspirational Voices and more!), this 90-minute concert event will celebrate the freedoms guaranteed by our First Amendment—of speech, religion, press, assembly, and protest—and the people power that both fuels these rights and is essential to guarantee them.

### Join us for a joyful Watch Party

Streaming at [afterthoughts](https://www.afterthoughtsvt.com/), starting at 7:30 pm. Come early to enjoy a cash bar before the singing begins.  

### If you can't join our Watch Party

[Watch from home](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdb638mWKXDrNOaHujbrGdW8OpPdncxV0lSIc6G-MNN90FZjQ/viewform?pli=1) or wherever you are, sing along, and find inspiration in the performances. Stream begins at 7:30 p.m. ET and runs approximately 90 minutes.
