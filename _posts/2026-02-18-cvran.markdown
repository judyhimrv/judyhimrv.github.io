### Team IMRV worked with CVRAN on this fundraising effort

Throughout the month of March, IMRV team members sent daily emails illustrating their artistic process and the end results.  It was wonderful to see the range of artwork from our contributing members, along side fascinating write-ups and videos of each artist's process.

In the end, Team **IMRV raised $4,200** towards **CVRAN's total of just over $95,000**, which will go toward welcoming and providing support to refugees, immigrants, asylum seekers, and migrant workers living in, working in, and visiting Central Vermont.
